Our Vision

HIMGAE is leading a revolutionary green initiative, leveraging advanced algae biotechnology to address some of the world's most pressing challenges. Our visionary approach integrates carbon negative biodiesel production, sustainable aquaculture feed, and nature based carbon capture, creating a closed loop system that maximizes environmental and economic benefits.

Our Key Objectives

  • Establish Nepal's first carbon negative biodiesel facility to lead the charge in renewable energy production.
  • Produce high quality, sustainable aquaculture feed to enhance food security and promote responsible consumption.
  • Pioneer nature based carbon capture technology to significantly mitigate climate change impacts.
  • Drive local economic growth and job creation to foster social and economic development.
  • Lead groundbreaking research into Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) to pave the way for a low carbon aviation future.

Our Approach

  • Plant Cloning: We utilize advanced plant cloning techniques to propagate high yield bamboo varieties with consistent quality and superior carbon sequestration capacity. This ensures scalable, uniform biomass production across diverse geographies, significantly accelerating deployment and maximizing carbon removal efficiency.
  • Vertical Photobioreactors: Optimized for Nepal's tropical climate, our advanced cultivation system maximizes algae growth while minimizing land and water use, ensuring efficient and sustainable production.
  • Closed Loop Production: Our integrated process ensures near zero waste, with every byproduct becoming a valuable input for another process, exemplifying the principles of a circular economy.
  • Cutting Edge Research: Our ongoing R&D in strain optimization and Sustainable Aviation Fuel positions HIMGAE at the forefront of algae biotechnology, driving innovation and sustainability.
  • Community Empowerment: Through comprehensive training programs and job creation, we empower local communities, drive social development, and build capacity in sustainable practices.
